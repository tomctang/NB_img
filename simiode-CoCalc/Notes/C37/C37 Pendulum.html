C37 Pendulum

Assumptions

  1. F→=ma→\overrightarrow{F} = m \overrightarrow{a}F=ma (Newton's seonc law of motion)

  2. F→=F→gravity+F→rod+F→drag+F→external\overrightarrow{F} = \overrightarrow{F}_{\text{gravity}} + \overrightarrow{F}_{\text{rod}} + \overrightarrow{F}_{\text{drag}} + \overrightarrow{F}_{\text{external}}F=Fgravity​+Frod​+Fdrag​+Fexternal​ (forces are additive and these are the only ones in this situation)

  3. F→gravity=mg(0,−1)\overrightarrow{F}_{\text{gravity}} = mg(0, -1)Fgravity​=mg(0,−1) (gravity is a constant downward pull)

  4. F→rod \overrightarrow{F}_{\text{rod}} Frod​ is such that the distance between pivot and bob is constant

  5. F→drag=−bv→\overrightarrow{F}_{\text{drag}} = -b\overrightarrow{v}Fdrag​=−bv (air and pivot resistance)

  6. F→external=Fcos⁡(ωt)(0,1)\overrightarrow{F}_{\text{external}} = F \cos(\omega t) (0,1)Fexternal​=Fcos(ωt)(0,1) (action on the pivot)
